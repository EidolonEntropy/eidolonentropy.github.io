You Can't Hide - Halloween MAP

About

You can run, but you can't hide! This MAP is an homage to the spirit of the Halloween season: horror, fear, and violence. Each part is presented not only to the viewer, but also to an in-universe audience of each participant's sonas!

Any fandom that fits the theme, as well as AUs and OCs, are welcome in this MAP! Horror stories, creepy characters, and frightening imagery are all encouraged. Blood and gore are also permitted.

MAP History

This project was originally hosted by Withersia. The project was picked up by EidolonEntropy when the original host was unable to continue it.

This MAP opens with the participants' sonas entering a haunted mansion. As they explore, they repeatedly encounter EidolonEntropy mascot Entoz'ia, showing them the 'wonders of the mansion' through paper cutouts. The bulk of this MAP is essentially this concept: telling a story through paper cutouts of the characters, similar to this.

This MAP is exclusively PMV and should have little to no AMV elements. A playlist of parts with the intended style can be found here.

Technical Aspects

Character Choice Restrictions

Nonfiction / real life concepts and certain problematic fandoms are disallowed; you will be warned of such if you attempt to apply with them.

Catified or similar characters are allowed.

No two participants can use the same fandom. Please check the parts list in the description before applying!

Your sona, dressed up in their Halloween costume, will be featuring in other parts of the video, so please do not use your sona in your part! Additionally, consider what you want said costume to be, because you cannot also use that character/creature/etc. in your part.

Part Formatting
  • 16x9 aspect ratio and no smaller than 720p: common pixel formats are 1440x720, 1920x1080, 2880x1620, and 3840x2160. The final project will be 3840x2160.
  • At least 24fps. The final project will be 60fps.
  • No personal watermarks (they will be added to the final project)
  • No program watermarks, no exceptions
  • High quality background music is not required
  • Green/colored screen is allowed
  • Try to avoid interlacing/ghosting
Submitting your part

Upload your final part to MyAirBridge via the 'Send Files Via Link' option (you don't need email!) and include this link when you submit your part to Discord and/or in the description of your video. Do not put the link in a comment.

It is preferred that your submitted part is either exactly as long as the part shown in the call video or a bit longer, without any extra cutoffs.

The version you upload to YouTube does not need to be identical to this one. You can add cutoffs and watermarks to the version on YT if you'd like.

Visuals

Art Direction

See the MAP call for various examples, but ignore the [REDACTED] parts' imagery!

All characters and certain scene elements are done in full color and must be encased in a blocky white border (aka paper cutout). These elements have black drop shadows.

Backgrounds are meant to look like plain paper with elements vaguely sketched onto it. There is NO COLOR in backgrounds, only rough black outlines. See this part for a good background example.

Shading is pure black cell ONLY. Do not add highlights.

Include the codeword 'hey' in your comment along with the codeword from the Signup Details slide.

Various Rules
  • Try to avoid any type of blurring or distortion effects when editing.
  • Backgrounds shouldn't get too detailed. Think of it like doodling on a piece of printer paper with a ballpoint pen.
  • Blood can be regular colored and encased in a cutout, or, if it's part of the background, it should conform to the background rules (black and sketchy, no cutout).
  • Backgrounds should not change in brightness.
  • Do NOT include lyrics in your part.
  • The black spots/stars/dust present in the backgrounds of the call arts is not a requirement, just a coincidence.

Signup Details

Due Dates

You have three weeks from your acceptance date to finish your part. Extensions can be given if a valid WIP is provided. While I won't let your work go to waste, I must be strict enough to ensure the MAP's timely completion.

As Halloween gets closer, due dates will get weird. In a perfect world, all parts will be done by the 28th. If it is less than three weeks until the 28th, the 28th is your permanent deadline. Don't join if you don't think you'll finish.

Discord

This MAP has a Discord sever so contact is easier, since this is a very time sensitive project. Joining is optional but highly recommended. You can post WIPs and ask questions more easily through the server.

Join the server here due to an influx of unwanted users, join links will be handed out upon acceptance.

By signing up for my MAP and providing me content, you give me full rights to edit, reuse, repost, and/or deny the usage of your work. You must trust I will credit you appropriately.
Comment Formatting

This is not first come first served, the channel you comment with will be searched for your animation experience. Explaining your part and the story you plan to portray increases your acceptance chances. Please indicate if your part will contain heavy spoilers!

When commenting, include the codeword 'Halloween'. Your comment will be disregarded if this codeword is absent.

You can have up to two parts at a time, but this is open for discussion.
