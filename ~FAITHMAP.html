FAITH

72hr Dark Love Palette MAP

Dark Love: when an individual expresses their admiration in immoral ways.

FAITH is a love letter to all our favorite nasty lovers and their evil ways, accentuating their corrupt behavior through a striking palette. As the song progresses, it goes from seemingly innocent to outright bloody.

Featured in this MAP are original characters, alternate universe characters, and fandom characters from a variety of different media.

Most participants did great adhering to the 72 hour deadline, but some snafus on our end delayed the MAP's release by a year, however, this enabled a Valentine's Day release and a project that turned out much better than it would've been if it had been rushed out any sooner. Thank you to all for your patience.

A huge thank you to all the participants! Only your participation made this possible!
