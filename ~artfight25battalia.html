FEATURED - Takeover In The Name of Love //

These are characters in a 100% original story about power, rule, and the importance of happiness, told through dragons because dragons are cool

  • Rectrix Battalia
    A discouraged former grandmaster finds company in a group of revolutionaries, but her desire for something greater will never be quelled.
  • Aviatrix Eclipse
    A strange dragonet of stranger origins. When his closest friend and mentor goes missing, he feels it's his responsibility to find her, no matter what it takes.
  • Agitatrix Archetto
    A freedom-obsessed cynic, Archetto cares only about his ideals. If anything comes between them, he will not hold back.
  • Gladiatrix Dahlia
    One of the Army's youngest members, Dahlia was raised in the group. She is just as amazing a friend as she is a fighter.
  • Curatrix Ocelot
    The head scientist of the Red Army. He's snarky and sarcastic; he's brilliant, but other dragons like Archetto simply can't stand him.
  • NEW! Aviatrix Miracle
    Don't misjudge Mira for their diminutive stature and softspokenness - they are clever, careful, and a fierce friend.
    Has no attacks!
  • Red Leader
    The supreme leader of the Red Army, a group looking to restore the rightful regime. His damaged right forelimbs are replaced with magitek cybernetics.
  • Rectrix Pronghorn
    This scarred commander and trainer is among the most respected individuals of the Red Army. Renowned for his skills with a spear.
    Has no attacks!
  • Curatrix Integrity
    The mysterious head medic of the Red Army. The Curatrix is willfully mute and shares no details with anyone.
    Has no attacks!
  • Gladiatrix Ray
    An abrasive individual with anger issues he's slowly getting over. Commissioned enchanted armor from other Army members.
    Has no attacks!
  • Regina Nevan
    Ex-Grandmaster of the Night Legion and Battalia's mother. A poor ruler, and increasingly paranoid near the end, but she was one of the better ones.
    Has no attacks!
  • Emerald
    The head of the friend group once consisting of himself, Margay, Tarn and Torture. A wisecracking artist with heart.
  • Margay
    Best friend of Emerald's whose favorite thing in the world is himself. Endlessly positive and gushing with energy.
  • Tarn
    Once Red Leader's best friend, now his sworn enemy. Tarn does not forgive nor forget, and he is now the sole resistance to the Red Army.
If you want to attack a Takeover character that's not listed here, you can by targeting the Additional Characters profile. Unless otherwise stated, I do not want them drawn alongside non-Takeover characters.

Duality of a Wolf //

The EidolonEntropy mascot also has a story! Now with the whole nuclear family themselves!

  • Entoz'ia
    Born as a split brained individual, both halves grew up separately until the one fateful day when their family fell apart and their brain reunited; however, the halves remained separate through DID. The noble leader of their pack.
  • Shizuka
    The abusive mother of Entoz'ia completely lost her memory as the result of a terrible injury. A completely changed wolf, she would never recover and slowly succumb to dementia and insanity.
  • NEW! Blackstar
    The alpha of the pack and Entoz'ia's father. All fear him. No one dares question his reign - until some unpopular views come to light, and his mate loses faith in him.
  • NEW! Epinyct
    Entoz'ia's older sibling: Epinyct follows in the footsteps of Blackstar, hoping to please him by becoming a great fighter. Few hear Epinyct speak.
    Has no attacks!
  • NEW! Endo'geny
    Entoz'ia's younger sibling: this headstrong pup is constantly getting into trouble. Endo'geny is a free spirit brought down by the family's prejudices.
    Has no attacks!
  • Tsara
    Originally sent to assassinate the leader of Entoz'ia's pack, she became attached to Entropy, but unbeknownst to her, this is the wolf she was sent to kill. She later becomes the pack's deputy.
  • Bobert
    Absolute scrunkle who sniffs you while you sleep. Chaotic neutral at its finest.
    Has no attacks!
  • Vergil
    Dedicated power hungry loner. He is the storm that is approaching.
    Has no attacks!
  • One
    Feral version. Poses as a friendly persona until his true power and motives break loose.
    Has no attacks!
These characters are complex, but fun to delve into. If you're not into the lore, the Additional Characters profile is the way to go.
