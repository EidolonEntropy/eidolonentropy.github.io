Part 1

EidolonEntropy Battalia and Myth

"First of all, I would like to give a massive thank you to Battalia. I had fallen behind in school and as the quarter's end neared, I had to shift all focus to completing all the missing work I had, rendering me unable to complete my parts (1-2). She then took what I had finished/planned and completed the parts for me and I cannot thank her enough. Here, as the scene opens we see a spider, nicknamed SpÏder, just chilling in their web. The reason why I had this occur was because I thought it'd be a fun way to introduce/begin the MAP as it plays off the title and actually has it in the web. When planning the parts, I also wanted to build up some spooky tension, especially the one created by Part 1's audio. Thus why in Part 1 it slowly pans away from the spider to only show an empty field waving in the wind while a deer, jokingly nicknamed Louis by Battalia and I, stands there, hopefully creating an unnerving feeling in viewers."
(Continued in the next page...)

Part 2

EidolonEntropy Battalia and Myth

"As the deer enters the forest, I wanted to continue to play into this unnerving feeling, but make it scarier-more shocking to hopefully create some terror in the viewers in the good name of Halloween. So, I planned to have the deer trot through the forest a bit, playing off the chiller music to create a more relaxed mood so that when the shadowy figure, a formerly-human, cursed entity being punished for their crimes (note: they are not supposed to be a representation of a a w/n/d/g/o), suspense is recreated, but hopefully in a stronger manner. And as a result, when the deer is suddenly killed there is a strong feeling of shock instilled."

M//EidolonEntropy

Part 8

Battalia of EidolonEntropy

"The first characters I thought of when designing this MAP's color palettes weren't OCs at all; they were these two, Cat Noir and Cat Blanc, from the animated cartoon Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug and Cat Noir. Although we had always intended for this to be an OC MAP, I just had to make an exception...
"I always like stories where a dark side of a character is haunting themselves; see the Nightmare's Lullaby MAP. So, although these two would never meet, and likely the canon-timeline Noir will never know Blanc existed, I wanted to play with Blanc representing Noir's potential to turn to the dark, that he's still human, and if it all goes wrong, Blanc can rise again."

B//EidolonEntropy

Part 11

YourFruity Skrill

"When I was looking through the parts for the first time, part 11 really 'caught my eye'. The music box-like theme really reminded me of a puppet theatre or circus and something related to amusement parks. This was also an experiment with stylization and designing, to fit the atmosphere and theme of the part.
"The plot, where a puppeteer plays with their puppet came to my mind and I decided to use Static (white-eyed wolf) for it. He is a robot, and he has his own AI - Adrie (aka Adrenaline; black wolf). The idea of Adrie controlling Static, who is just entertaining people, was something I came up with nearly immediately. Adrie is the representation of Static's darker side, the gloomy depths of his consciousness, his deepest fears."

Along Came A Spider

Halloween Palette MAP

You're in for a treat.

What better way to celebrate Halloween than an animation set to the song that embodies the dark, spooky nature of the holiday?

Way back in mid-2019, Jaxorri and I collaborated on hosting this MAP, with the call originally going up on their channel. Unfortunately, we did not receive enough parts in time, so this year, I rehosted it individually, and with a few tweaks to the original call. This time around, enough parts were accounted for to create this outstanding final product!

A huge thank you to all the participants, direct and indirect! Only your exceptional participation made this possible!
