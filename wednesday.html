ATTACHED CONTENT

In order to make more 3D portfolio fodder I needed to make a prop for a rig of mine: I went into this intending to make a version of Vaingloria’s swords similar to the character model itself, something low poly, low effort, kinda garbage all around. Turns out I can’t model something bad on purpose. Almost laughably so. These swords are 4x as many polygons as the character.

I guess that means the only logical step is to make a neat render showcasing them because I spent too long on them. This is probably the best this model will ever look with full textures and lights (it still looked better in Waking The Witch… and Picture Day too) and it’s unlikely I’ll attempt to make it look nice ever again lmao. At least the swords are cool
