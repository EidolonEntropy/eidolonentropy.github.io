WELCOME TO THE WORLD - OC Palette MAP

About

Achtung Bitte! You like opportunities to animate your characters to a fun song with a fun color palette with 22 other people? Us too.

This MAP is cohosted by Kassian and B//EidolonEntropy.

This MAP is about having fun, intended for those troublemaking characters and their antics. Have fun with it!

This is a palette MAP, and there are two palettes used. As indicated in the MAP call, the palette used depends on the part.

 

This MAP should be upbeat and as vibrant as the color palette. It can tell a story or be purely abstract, like the inspiration for the call video, the JJBA OP Bloody Stream.

Here's a handy link to the lyrics of the song, if you need them. Don't include them in your part.

Technical Aspects

Animation Style

Any animation style is allowed, as long as it's fast paced and goes to the beat of the song. Limited animation is encouraged and tweening is discouraged.

Here's a playlist of parts handpicked to show the desired style.

Do not shade.

It's extremely important that the palette is followed as closely as possible.

Include the codeword 'hey' in your comment. Without it you won't be considered.

Part Formatting
  • 16x9 aspect ratio: common pixel formats are 1440x720, 1920x1080, 2880x1620, and 3840x2160. The final project will be in 4K.
  • At least 24fps (video, not animation!). The final project will be 60fps.
  • Try your best to match the colors as exact as possible. If they're too far off, your part will be edited.
  • No personal watermarks (they will be added to the final project)
  • No program watermarks
  • High quality background music is not required
  • Green/Red screen is allowed (choose the color that's not in your part)
Submitting your part

Upload your final part to MyAirBridge via the 'Send Files Via Link' option and include this link when you submit your part to Discord and/or in the description of your video. Do not put the link in a comment.

It is preferred that your submitted part is either exactly as long as the part shown in the call video or a bit longer, without any extra cutoffs.

The version you upload to YouTube does not need to be identical to this one. You can add cutoffs and watermarks to the version on YT if you'd like.

Palette

Refer to these examples for how to color your part, but refer to the palette image to pick your colors.

Various Rules
  • You can only use one palette in your part, and it must be the one shown in the MAP call during your part.
  • Gradients and halftones are allowed.
  • Lineart must be black. Outside the lineart, black is not allowed.
  • The off-white and off-black shown in the palette image are usable with both palettes.
  • If your foreground blends in too much with your background, add a thicker outline to your foreground in your editor.

Signup Details

Due Dates

You have three months from your acceptance date to finish your part. In three weeks we will ask for a WIP. Extensions can be given if a valid WIP is provided.

We will try our best to work with you to make sure your work doesn't go to waste. Please request backup if you don't think you'll be able to finish if you take a part now.

Discord

This MAP has a Discord sever so contact is easier. Joining is optional but highly recommended. You can post WIPs and ask questions more easily through the server.

Join the server here. Only join if you have been accepted for a part or for backup.

By signing up for our MAP and providing us content, you give us full rights to edit, reuse, repost, and/or deny the usage of your work. You must trust we will credit you appropriately.
Comment Formatting

This is not first come first served, the channel you comment with will be searched for your animation experience.

Parts will not be chosen until the call is up for about 12 hours.

The intro, outro, and Part -1 are valid parts. The time marked by '-' in the video is not a part.

Include the previously mentioned codeword.

You can have up to two parts.
