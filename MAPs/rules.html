General information for Battalia's MAPs

Before You Join...

  • My projects are NOT beginner friendly! If your channel does not have three or more examples of your work publicly visible, please provide a link to where I can find them.*
  • An off-platform means of contact is required. Joining the provided Discord server is recommended but not mandatory. If not using Discord, please provide an alternative means of contact. I want to know your current progress as often as possible!
  • It is my job as host to create the best possible final product. This means participants should expect to receive some degree of input on their part, and minor changes may be required. If you are allergic to criticism, my projects are not for you.
  • That being said, it is also my job as host to ensure no work is put to waste. I am always receptive to a participant's current situation and I will never drop someone on the verge of completion. My number one reason for dropping participants is inactivity.
  • I do not have an explicit blacklist for fandoms/content/etc. but I reserve the right to deny an idea if it violates the spirit of the project.
  • I will attempt to preserve the artist's intent in finished parts as much as possible, but I reserve the right to make small edits to a part in the final project.

Submission Requirements

  • Minimum: 1280x720 @ 24fps
  • Recommended: 3840x2160 @ 60fps
  • Final part files must be delivered through MyAirBridge (link sharing) or a similar file sharing service that does not compress files
  • All video formats accepted (within reason)
  • Transitions (aka greenscreen) allowed; where applicable a PNG sequence is preferred
  • No watermarks, programs or personal
  • High quality background music not required, but audio is recommended to ensure proper timing
  • Avoid codecs with interlacing/ghosting
  • No cutoffs necessary; please cut your part slightly before it starts and slightly after it ends, to ensure no gaps appear between parts
  • Include two exclamation marks in your comment as acknowledgement of reading this document
