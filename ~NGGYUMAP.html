Never Gonna Give You Up

72hr Spoof MAP ft. Winter and Moon

That's right, you've just been WinterRolled.

Dreamed up by Cloakd and brought to life by B//EidolonEntropy, this MAP is the result of fifteen unique creators uniting under the love for a meme (and pity for a specific IceWing).

This project was meant to put an ironic spin on the song. Winter, though infatuated by Moon, doesn't know the meaning of commitment.

This MAP begun accepting participants on the 31st of January, and was completed just over a week later on the 8th of February, making it arguably the quickest WoF Spoof MAP to be completed. Each participant did very well adhering to their 72 hour deadline with very few exceptions, creating a masterpiece in so little time.

A huge thank you to all the participants! Only your participation made this possible!
