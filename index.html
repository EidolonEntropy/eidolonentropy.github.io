Battalia is the primary content creator for and the founder of EidolonEntropy. The artist-animator creates most of EidolonEntropy's content featuring a range of techniques spanning both 2D and 3D methods. Battalia specialises in a wide variety of different content types including voice/audio, video editing, web design, and the technical side of animation.

Battalia is the author of TAKEOVER: In The Name Of Love, a story of meaning and happiness told through the lens of cool dragons.

Unless otherwise stated, most work done under EidolonEntropy is by Battalia, the webmaster. Thumbnails and other items are watermarked with the red left facing insignia.

While Eidolon is acceptable, the names Battalia (B//EidolonEntropy) or SCP are preferable.

Legal

Under fair use laws, no matter if you credit us or not, our work can be used if you are using it for criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, and/or research purposes. This doesn't stop you from crediting, but you are not required to. Using our work transformatively is safer.

Additionally, we permit the use of our work for visual entertainment purposes such as profile pictures, printed stills, and animator tributes without credit, so long as you do not pass it off as your own work. You are not allowed to say the work is yours. It is preferred to say it belongs to us, but we don't require it.

We do NOT allow our work to be plagiarized without direct credit. This means you cannot trace, copy, or otherwise edit any of our work, drawn or written, without giving a direct link to our original source work. You must say your work was based on work by EidolonEntropy.

In short:

You are allowed to use our work without credit for...
- uses permitted under fair use (criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, and research)
- profile pictures/banners
- printing physical copies for your enjoyment
- animator tributes/other compilations
...though it's nice of you to include credit.
You are not allowed to use our work if you...
- trace it
- copy it
- edit it
...without providing a link to the original source and crediting EidolonEntropy.

Additionally, our work can be used as stated above no matter what it features. For example, if we did an art trade with another person that features their character, you can use it as your profile picture without credit.

It is also not required that you ask before using our work.

If you have any questions on if your usage is legal, please send a message via any link on the front page or here.
