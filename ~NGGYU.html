Rules for the Never Gonna Give You Up Spoof MAP


Never Gonna Give You Up - Winter & Moon Spoof MAP

Rules

Reading this document is mandatory for receiving a part!


  • Tracing, copying, and/or stealing work, as well as NSFW or otherwise inappropriate content, is strictly prohibited!
  • AMV or PMV is allowed, although PMV is preferred.
  • Video frame size must be 16:9, preferably 1080p or up. Any PMV elements must have a framerate greater than or equal to 15fps.
  • This MAP is not first come first served.
  • Once you receive your part, you have 72 hours to complete it. You can ask for a 12 hour extension, but if you don’t finish by then, you will be kicked. Codeword 1 - never gonna let you down
  • Only FlipAClip watermarks are allowed. Do not add your own.
  • You can only have one part.
  • You can sign up for backup at any time. Codeword 2 - if you're requesting a part, let us know what you’re planning for your part. If you're requesting backup, tell us your favorite part(s).
  • You must follow the designs given, which can be found here. You can use the search button or Ctrl-F to find specific characters easily.
  • Your part must be fully colored.
  • Winter and Moon should be the only characters in the foreground. Other characters should only feature in the background.
  • Make sure Winter is extremely happy and enthusiastic, and trying to act suave. Moon should look annoyed, like she just wants to get out of there.
  • There doesn’t have to be lipsync, but make sure you can tell it’s Winter singing.
  • This song was chosen for the irony of it, and you can show that in your part. For example, if the lyrics were 'never gonna say goodbye' you could have Winter flying away from Moon, like in the end of Moon Rising.
