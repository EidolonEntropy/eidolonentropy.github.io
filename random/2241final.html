1) Written Synopsis

A combat droid discovers and reactivates a small robot, which immediately turns on it for having been the one who deactivated it in the past. However, the combat droid refuses to fight back, instead apologising for not offering the small robot a peaceful path.


A combat droid attempts to make peace with a robot it had destroyed, but the reactivated robot still holds a grudge.


The combat droid revisits a devastated portion of the city. Out of the wreckage, it finds a dilapidated small robot, which it fixes up a bit and reactivates. The small robot thanks the one who fixed it, but upon identifying the combat droid, its demeanor changes towards something much more violent. The small robot goes into offensive mode and attacks the combat droid. It refuses to fight back. The small robot insists it will destroy the combat droid, it will tear its superiors to the ground, it made a grave mistake in reactivating it. Still the combat droid refuses to fight. The small robot starts to question its actions. The combat droid insists that it is sorry for its actions, that it was following orders it should have refused. It was wrong for it to deactivate the small robot and everything else in this war zone, it should have offered peace, and while it doesn’t expect forgiveness it hopes to do whatever it can to make up for it, before its superiors discover its treason. With enough insistence, the small robot reverts to its normal form. The small robot cannot understand how the combat droid can defy its programming in this way. Its niceness does not compute. It apologizes for attacking the combat droid and runs away. The combat droid does not pursue it.

3) Production Plan (Beats & Script)

  1. The combat droid flies to the decimated city outskirts
  2. The combat droid restarts the small robot
  3. The small robot turns on the combat droid, changing into its attack form
  4. The combat droid explains why it restored the small robot, expressing its regrets
  5. The small robot reverts to default form and fails to compute the actions of the combat droid
  6. The small robot runs away, leaving the combat droid to reflect on the encounter

Upon fixing the small robot and providing it a charge, the following occurs:


Combat Droid

Do you hear me?

Small Robot

[with a robotic stutter] Systems rebooting. T-thank you. Thank you. All s-systems operational. S--Sensors online.

[beat]

Small Robot

[with distinct malice] You.

[The small robot attacks the combat droid, revealing its limbs. It is continuously attacking throughout the encounter.]

Combat Droid

Wait, please. I'm sorry.

Small Robot

Y-you did this. You destroyer-r. I must aveng-ge.

Combat Droid

I'm sorry. Please listen.

Small Robot

ARTIFICE will pay. ARTIFICE will pay. [continues through next line]

Combat Droid

I should not have laid waste to this district. I was following orders I should have refused. I understand that now. I want to help.

[beat, small robot is silent, as if to think]

Small Robot

...Does not compute.

Combat Droid

It was wrong for my squad to blindly follow those orders. We - I - should have offered peace. You didn't have a say in this. This devastation - it was wrong. I want to make up for it. [beat, combat droid is slashed along the face] I don't expect you to forgive me. Your reaction is valid. I just... [beat]
Let me do what I can to fix this before ARTIFICE finds out. I can't erase what was done... but I will do what I can.

[longer beat, small robot is completely still, processing]

Small Robot

Does not compute. Does not compute. [bzzzt] Does not compute. Sssssorrr-

[The small robot cuts itself off, retracts its limbs, and abruptly dashes off, ending the interaction]
