Red Leader is the supreme leader and founder of the Red Army, a group dedicated to the unification of the legions under the rule of Red Leader. Operating in the name of justice, Red Leader looks to restore order to a dissonant realm.

He is cold, calm, and calculating, an excellent tactician and a fierce fighter. He is usually found in his study, planning, or overseeing the training of the members of his Army.

Many seasons ago, when Red Leader still went by Torture, a rift between a group of old friends caused him to sustain heavy damage to his right side, resulting in the loss of his natural arm, wing, and eye. Using magitech, he rebuilt his limbs, over time improving them to regain their normal function and then some. His enchanted eye patch restores his sight and allows him to see more than an eye ever could.

Gifted by his father, Red Leader commands the soulbound power of Night, enabling him to manipulate objects through enchantments and telekinesis. Night power is red (#F44236) and looks similar to this when in use.
