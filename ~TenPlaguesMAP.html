Queen Wasp

QUEEN WASP is the tyrannical ruler of the HiveWings and the oppressor of the SilkWings. She has the unique power to mind control all HiveWings whom she stings, giving her a powerful army comprised of unwitting everyday citizens. Believing SilkWings to be an inferior tribe, Wasp revels in her control over them, and instantly quashes any sign of rebellion - just like she did with the LeafWings, who were wiped out after threatening Wasp's rule.

The LeafWings

THE LEAFWINGS were killed off in the Tree Wars by Wasp and her army. At least, that's the official story. Some survivors have been living in secret, away from the Hives, plotting to take down the rule of the HiveWings no matter the cost. Their weapon: flamesilk. SUNDEW and her fellow revolutionaries plan to set fire to the hives, creating havoc to confuse Wasp's army. The LeafWings will then strike with the help of rebel SilkWings to find Wasp and force her to relinquish her rule.

The Chrysalis

THE CHRYSALIS are a clandestine group of SilkWings looking to overthrow Wasp through activism. By shattering the myth around Wasp's power, they hope to encourage their tribe to rise up against Wasp and take back their sovereignty. When the LeafWing attack began, The Chrysalis emerged to help fend off the queen's evil forces and help get innocent civilians out of the war zones.

Cricket

CRICKET is a young, bookish HiveWing integral to the revolution because, for reasons unknown, she is the only commoner HiveWing that cannot be mind controlled by Wasp. She can use her HiveWing privileges to help the Chrysalis, and is asked by Sundew to help the LeafWings acquire the flamesilk they need to set fire to the Hives. Cricket, however, laments the simple life she once had, and detests the violence she is complicit in.

Lady Jewel

LADY JEWEL is the leader of Jewel Hive, and a friend of the Chrysalis. Wasp's powers don't affect her cousin, allowing Jewel to speak her mind against Wasp's tyranny. She pleads to Wasp to stop the destruction, to surrender to stop the suffering.

Blue

BLUE is a young SilkWing whom just underwent his metamorphosis and learned that he has the rare ability to create flamesilk. He joined the Chrysalis to stay out of HiveWing custody, and has now become an integral part to Sundew's plan: they can only proceed if Blue ageees to offer his flamesilk to the cause, giving the LeafWings the weapon they need to burn down the Hives. He feels strongly about the independence of his tribe and confronted Wasp himself, demanding she let his people go.

Lady Scarab

LADY SCARAB is HiveWing royalty, but not a ruler of a Hive. She is harsh and just, but intensely dislikes Wasp. Though she doesn't care much for the revolution, she does want to see the downfall of Wasp, and confronts her once she sees how Wasp is reacting to the attack. Though Wasp can't mind control her, her words hurt just as much.

Swordtail

SWORDTAIL is a mischevious Chrysalis member and friend of Blue and Cricket. He is fully behind the LeafWing plan to burn down the Hives, and is quick to thrust himself into the heat of battle to save those he cares about.

Chrysalis Leaders

ESCARGOT is the leader of the Chrysalis. He's patient and cautious, but incredibly driven towards his goal of the downfall of HiveWing society. While he tries to avoid unnecessary conflict, he considers the dangers of the LeafWings' plan to be an unfortunate compromise if it means his tribe will be liberated.

ARCUS is a senior Chrysalis member and friend of Escargot. He is wary of the morality of the LeafWings' plot, and was integral to the Chrysalis's attempts to evacuate the Hives of all innocent SilkWings during the attack.

Escargot and company star in their own AU MAP, which is currently in progress!

Ten Plagues

Wings of Fire 3rd Arc AU MAP

After six long years of development, we are proud to finally present to you Ten Plagues! This project was conceived prior to the release of The Poison Jungle, when the Leaf/SilkWing Revolution was still a popular theory for where the books were going. This AU harkens back to a simpler plot for the Pantalan conflict, oozing nostalgia from 2019.

This MAP features both old and new blood from the community, with parts from 2019 beside parts from 2025. Our deepest thanks to all participants, contributors and eagerly waiting fans over these six years.
