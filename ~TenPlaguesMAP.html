Flawed Design

Darkstalker MAP

"And if I could control it, maybe I could leave it all behind..."

Hosted in 2018 and finished in 2019, cohosted by Battalia of EidolonEntropy and Henki, and brought to life by nineteen incredible artists, the Flawed Design MAP is a lesser known gem of the Wings of Fire animation community, portraying the faults in the life of Darkstalker before he was put to sleep for thousands of years.

Included on this page are each participant's individual part uploads (if they have one) and links to their channels. It would mean the world to each one if you could watch their uploads, visit their channels, and perhaps dish out some subscriptions!

The 2021 reedit restores parts affected by unnecessary edits. It fixes part transitions, corrects inconsistencies, removes a previously undetected levels edit, changes text effects and fonts, and updated outdated usernames, all wrapped up in a 4K 60fps experience. Most of these edits were based on viewer feedback through the experience survey and through comments. Thank you to everyone who provided suggestions for making this MAP and any others better.
